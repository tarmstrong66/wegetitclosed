\#\# Mortgage Compliance Rules

Do not say:

\- Guaranteed approval  
\- Guaranteed lowest rates  
\- Everyone qualifies  
\- No-cost loan  
\- Instant approval  
\- Close in X days guaranteed

Use language like:

\- May qualify  
\- Depending on your scenario  
\- Subject to credit, income, property, program, and lender guidelines  
\- Talk with a loan officer about your options  
\- Compare mortgage options based on your situation

Every loan approval, rate, term, payment, and program discussion should be framed as scenario-specific and subject to qualification.

\#\# Redirect Plan

Preserve pages with SEO value.

Redirect weak, outdated, duplicate, or removed pages to the closest matching authority page.
